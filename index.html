How do SI and Professional Services firms stay relevant
in the Cloud economy?

Options

Retain & Retrain

Retain and retrain existing consultants.  Target cloud development, integration, dev-ops.

Investment: Structured re-training program.
Revenue: Pro-serve fees net from retraining investments.

Productize

Productize high value IP. Setup Saas / MSP business unit.

Investment: Product development, product team.
Revenue: Licensing and Subscriptions.

Cloud Services Brokering

Partner with aligned cloud services companies and fulfill reseller and systems integration services.  Pipeline to create and productize high demand IP.

Investment: Partnership development, implementation teams.
Revenue:  Recurring licensing and pro-serv fees.
